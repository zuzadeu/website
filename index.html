About Me

As an ML engineer with five years of professional experience in a start-up, a research organization, and corporations, I see a lot of challenges in how artificial intelligence-related topics are being explained to a business by data science teams. Additionally, finding good tutorials in the developer community is not always effortless. Therefore, my main career goal apart from expanding my MLOps knowledge is broadly understood data storytelling.

As part of my personal development, I am currently studying coaching. I already have some experience both in presenting the possibilities of Artificial Intelligence to companies and in supporting recruitment processes. However, I would like to support not only companies but also individuals in terms of individual development in this field, because Data Science is an ambiguous term and it is easy to get lost in it. That is why I want to learn how to conduct a coaching process and improve my qualifications in the area of ​​soft skills and psychology.
